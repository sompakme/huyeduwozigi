10 Gifts To Get Your Friends Based On Their MBTI

 Posted on September 20, 2024  |  7 minutes  |  1324 words  |  Aldo Pusey

When buying for friends who you know well enough to know their MBTI, it's clear that the bond is pretty strong. For those special people who you know inside and out, gift-giving can be even more stressful. The more you know, the more choice you have and the harder it is to pick that one special item for a loved one.

These gifts are ideal choices for different personality types whether they be introverts, extroverts, thinkers or feelers. Let your loved ones know you love them for who they are and what their true nature makes them contribute to the world, with these gift ideas.

10 ESFP - Novelty Pizza Throw Blanket

Amazon

This is ideal for an ESFP type, or someone who is an enthusiastic and spontaneous lover of life and all that it has to offer - including, especially, material comforts like cozy blankets. This pizza throw is warm and luxurious, while still representing a fun-loving soul who likes to throw caution to the wind and follow their passion (for pizza).

The throw is about 60 inches in diameter with a stitched edge and a ridiculously realistic-looking pizza graphic on it. The blanket comes in a pizza box, perfectly packaged for gift-giving. This is a great gift for anyone really, as almost everyone loves pizza and being comfortable.

9 ENTP - Azul Board Game

Amazon

For personality types like ENTPs, a great gift is a group game that involves interesting strategy and being able to reason through patterns. This is a wonderful gift for someone who is good at reading people, as board games are always more fun when you can pick up on others' intentions.

This is an easy-to-learn game of drawing tiles and forming patterns and is a great option for a social gathering at the home of someone who is quick, alert and loves problem-solving. For extroverts who feel energized in groups, board games can be a way for them to hang out with groups including all personality types, as the game gives introverts something to focus on.

8 ISTJ - Bamboo Drawer Dividers

Amazon

These make wonderful additions to anyone who likes to be organized and responsible, keeping order in their life and environment.  ISTJ types and the like will benefit from these little gadgets to separate their kitchen, bedroom and bathroom drawers and maintain the logic, order and neatness that they crave in their life.

The dividers have rubber padded ends to protect furniture from scuffing, and they expand and retract to fit in various spaces. Bamboo is also a sustainable material, adding some eco-friendly kindness to this thoughtful and helpful gift. Help your friend jump on the Marie Kondo trend and get as organized as they can, for their own happiness.

7 INFJ - Wake Up to the Joy of You

Amazon

For a personality type who is interested in keeping harmony very present in their life, meditation and mindfulness are often key interests. For an INFJ type or anyone else who seeks meaning, connection and goodness in their life, this book is a great place to start with mindful reading.

The book delves into the building blocks of self-care, leading the reader on a journey of letting go of some things and keeping what's important in focus. The author is Agapi Stassinopoulos, a bestselling author and speaker. This is a great bedside book for early morning readings or little nuggets of wisdom to tuck into just before bedtime.

6 ISTP -  Mind Delights: Good-for-Your-Soul Puzzles and Activities

Amazon

This is a gently challenging activity book to keep minds sharp and occupied, and provide relaxing puzzles to people who thrive on problem-solving and thoughtful, quiet observation.

Personality types like ISTPs will find this book compelling and it gives them a nice thing to do when they are hanging out by themselves and recharging their batteries, as introverts need to do. The book is full of dot-to-dots, coloring pages, word-finds, mazes and other activities, all geared towards adults. It's sometimes hard to find a version of an activity book that is challenging enough for an adult but still relaxing for a nice break - this book is it.

5 ESTP - Insulated Wine Tumbler

Amazon

This is a way to keep wine nice and cool, without any risk of spillage during an outdoor party, picnic or fun barbecue with friends. The type of party hosted by an ESTP might call for one or a few of these vessels.

The designs are cosmic and colorful, with a wide variety to choose from. ESTP types are flexible and spontaneous, the type to grab a glass of wine and run right into the middle of a backyard volleyball game. Think ahead on their behalf and let them be their true, fun-loving, improvisational self with this tumbler sturdily supporting their drink at their side.

4 The Best Bar Trivia Book Ever

Amazon

If your friend is an ENTJ type or someone who loves to learn, expand on their mind, follow logic and act as a leader in organizing fun social events like trivia nights, this is a great gift. The book contains questions and answers to a multitude of trivia questions, on subjects such as pop culture, sports, history and science.

This is the type of gift your friend can casually pore over and study, expanding on their knowledge, or may inspire them to host a trivia night or a fun games night with friends. This is for brainiacs who are also social butterflies and love taking the reins and sharing facts.

3 ESTJ - Panda Planner

Amazon

The Panda Planner is a highly designed, scientifically strategized planner that aims to improve your life as you use it. The format calls for daily check-ins, as well as goal-setting for future months, weeks and days.

The planner combines an agenda with a gratitude journal and a goal planner, helping to guide the owner towards a more organized and happy life. This is an excellent gift for someone who is an ESTJ type or who thrives on organizing themselves and other people, getting things done that need to be done and making exciting plans. Your favorite ESTJ or similar type will feel loved and seen by this gift.

2 ENFP - Cranium

Amazon

Cranium is the ultimate game of ENFPs, who are full of creativity and imagination, as well as very intelligent and quick to make connections. They are energized by being around others and interacting with them, over molding clay, acting out silent clues and drawing with their eyes closed.

Their verbal dexterity and support for others both lend themselves to this fun guessing game. Cranium requires teams, so it's great for extroverts who like to host and attend board game nights and parties where they can exchange positive energy with their loved ones. This game is also very accessible and enjoyable for a wide range of ages and is a great gift for a family.

1 ISFP - Painting With Bob Ross

Amazon

This book is a step-by-step guide to learning to paint with oil, accompanied by Bob Ross content, references, methods and brush recommendations. A gift that encourages learning a new skill or doing a craft that can be performed during alone time is perfect for an ISFP, who is sensitive and present.

They enjoy taking their time with work and tasks, so having a hobby that works well with their patience is ideal. This is a great gift that lends itself to quiet self-reflection, peaceful me time and enjoying the here and now of the present moment. To be honest, Bob Ross does present as a bit of an ISFP himself.
