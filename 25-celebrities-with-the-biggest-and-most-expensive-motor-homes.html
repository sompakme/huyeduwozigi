We have talked about homes and mansions costing millions of dollars, same goes for expensive cars and yachts. These are favorites of rich people, most of whom usually enjoy the celebrity status. Still, one cannot bring out a yacht on the road so how does one show off their wealth? Well, that is where motor homes comes in. They have been around for quite some time now but the truth is that just like everything else, there are cheaper motor homes and then there are the more expensive one.

The basic concept of a motor home is to provide you with a space to live when you're out on the move without having to setup up a camping tent everywhere you go. All you need to do is park somewhere and you're all set. Luckily for celebrities, they can get some really expensive motor homes out there that not only carry the basic amenities necessary for living but are also decked out with expensive and luxury items like leather couches, entertainment systems and even entire recording studios. So, we set out and decided to search for some of the most expensive and biggest motor homes out there and we were definitely not disappointed. So, here are 25 celebrities who own some of the nicest and most expensive motor homes out there.

Brad Pitt’s Mansion On The Road

Brad Pitt’s motorhome is worth $1 million. It is 48 foot long and so spacious that it can accommodate his six kids. The professional kitchen is the highlight of this motorhome as the kitchen alone cost $60,000 to be crafted. The living room is huge with a 10-foot long couch where kids usually spend their time watching TV, and not only that, there is also a master bedroom.

The size of this motorhome has gained the attention of people from all over the globe. It serves all the possible things for the big family of Brad Pitt and he is known to take his kids on the motorhome out to his movie shoots. It has been designed with the best quality materials available and includes four pullouts to make it more wide and comfortable.

Robby Gordon’s Incredible Motor Home

Robbie Gordon is yet another celebrity who is the owner of a big and expensive motorhome. Being a car racer, he has very good taste in cars. He also has a lot of knowledge of cars and their specifications and same is the case with his motor home.

It has one of the most modern interiors and carries a very sleek design. Not only that, it is also perfect for going on off-road trips and is very powerful, durable as well as very strong. There will be nothing wrong in stating that this is indeed a home away from home because it is equipped with all the facilities to comfort a person. Being a record holder racer, Gordon has quite a collection of expensive cars and this luxurious motor home only adds to this collection.

John Legend’s King Ride

The Beauty and The Beast music star John Legend whose non-professional name is John Roger Stephens owns one of the most modern, stylish and unique motor homes out there. He is a pretty rich guy as he owns a house that is estimated to be worth around $14 million and not only that, he is also a 9-time Grammy award winner so having an expensive motor home is not that big of a deal for him.

This motor home is responsible for taking him everywhere with style and luxury. These rich people usually hire the best automobile company, engineers and designers to make sure that everything in their motorhome is perfect and better than any other motorhome out there. Legend usually does his off-shoot meetings in the mobile home and likes to stay inside it when he is away from home for shootings and performances.

Tommy Lee’s Road Master

The motorhome of Tommy Lee is famously known as ‘Scandalous’ and it is one of the more unique motorhomes out there. The life of celebrities is known to be very glamorous as they usually own million dollar properties and they all are in competition to show off their wealth. A motorhome is one such luxury which is why these days all the celebrities are in the race of having the most luxurious motor home.

Similar to other celebrities, Tommy Lee also has spent a large amount of his wealth towards buying a stunning, upgraded, and customized motor home. He is a musician by profession and pretty much everyone is aware of the fact that his songs always end up hitting millions of dollars in profit for him, so owning such an expensive and huge motor home isn't that big of a deal.

Gary Allan’s Tour Bus

Every celebrity has their own interest. Some invest on building huge palaces while others own the world’s most luxurious and expensive car. Gradually, however, the trend has shifted towards owning a fancy looking motor home so that the celebrity can make themselves comfortable between the breaks or after or before the show.

Gary Allan, a well known personality in the world of music is also listed for having an expensive motor home. He owns a matte black bus that you can often see at the site of his performances for album shoots. He travels every where on his tour bus, keeping all his necessary things with him. To make his bus look different and fascinating he has done some custom art on his kitchen and living room walls, which does an excellent job of grabbing everyone’s attention.

Johnny Cash's J.C Unit One

This motor home was owned by Johnny Cash and it is named as ‘J.C Unit One’. Johnny spent $500,000 on this customized motor home. The front lounge has a black leather couch and a television. This is the place where friends and family used to spend most of their time.

There are three private rooms which are decorated with many antique pieces that make it very unique when compared to other motor homes out there. In one of these rooms, there is a hickory wood table which was used during the civil war and Cash had also carved a ‘C’ on the table top. The private room of his wife was called “The Royal Box” and it is decorated with blue and white lace curtains. The motorhome is now on display for fans of Johnny Cash who can take a look at it, and on certain occasions even take a tour inside.

Unbelievable Motor Home of Gwen Stefani

Gwen Stefani is a well-known TV personality and also the owner of a fancy masterpiece of a motor home. This specific vehicle shows an awesome aesthetic sense and hard work of the designer. It has three sliders that double the space of this tour bus and makes it look like a luxurious hotel. The interior stands out greatly as it has been decorated in quite a beautiful manner.

The kitchen has a large counter and the metal background wall is the highlight and it also contains a wine chiller which is something you usually do not find in a motor home. The floor is of marble which helps keep it clean and not only that, it has a beautiful lounge, a large bedroom with a really comfortable adjustable bed, the setting of which you can adjust according to how much comfort you want. A large shower and bathroom are also present in this luxurious travel home.

Dramatic Tour Bus Of Kevin Hart

Next on our list is the exclusive motor home of Kevin Hart. It is one of his most prized possessions which is why he keeps his motor home very neat and tidy and addresses his guests to do it as well. Whenever he comes on the sets he likes to travel in his 1200 square feet gigantic tour bus.

It has two lounge areas with a 3D TV, an amazing customized make up station, closets, four bathrooms and a kitchen. Plus it has all the latest technology gadgets with the addition of an upgraded workspace as well. The upper portion is designed with glass windows everywhere. It is perfect for relaxing and taking a sunbath as well while the interior is also stunning. Being a celebrity, Hart sometimes needs to escape when fans surround his bus so there is an escape door as well.

Mobile Recording Studio Of Timbaland

We doubt that there are many people out there who aren't aware of the hip hop star Timbaland and there is no surprise in knowing that he has his own luxurious motor home. The really cool part about this motor home in particular is that along with some of the other luxury amenities, it also houses a recording studio inside it.

This means that Timbaland can easily record new music on the go without having to step out of the luxury of his expensive vehicle. That alone makes things a lot easier for him and allows him to save up some time to focus on other things. Other than the studio, the motor home includes a bedroom and an area for Timbaland and his crew to hangout. We can say that this is easily one of the coolest motor homes out there.

Shaquille’s Majestic Trailer

People that are close to him call him ‘Shaq’. Sports lover surely know him by this name too since he is a retired basketball player and he is no ordinary player either, oh no, he is the fifth highest scoring NBA player and this has allowed him to make a lot of money and gain a lot of fame.

He is huge guy and therefore he owns a huge motor home as well, its only natural after all. This motor home is super-sized and he ended up buying it by spending a large amount of money. How much you ask, well, it is estimated to have cost him around $161,700. It is a black and silver lined tour bus whose sides extend out to increase the inside space, making it more comfortable and all the luxury amenities are present inside.

‘The Ride’ Of Zac Brown

Similar to Timbaland, Zac Brown has also ensured that his motor home is a multipurpose vehicle that only serves as a luxury home on the go but also as a recording studio on wheels, allowing him to record his hits on the go.

He has named his motor home ‘The Ride’. On the inside, the flooring is made of beautiful marble while the living room has been customized with brown leather couches on which him and his crew can relax. Not only this, the living room has been decorated with pictures and many guitars are also placed on the walls so that there is no doubt about the fact that this motor home is owned by a musician. It has a bunk bed, a small kitchen with a microwave and some cabinets to keep things and there is a corner for playing music as well, obviously. For the exterior, the motor home has a sleek design and carries a shiny black paint job on the outside.

Amy Grant's Palace On Wheels

The Queen of Christian pop has also made our list of owners with the biggest and most expensive motor homes. When you have been blessed with so much, then why not spend it on luxury and comfort. Amy Grant has already enjoyed a life of fame, having been awarded with plenty of awards and because of this, she easily earns more than your average person.

The usual concept that women don't know much about classy automobiles is wrong and Amy Grant is proof of this. She owns one of the most classy looking motor homes around and it has all been built according to her very own requirements. The interior, as well as exterior, is up to the mark. It has a private lounge where most of her close friends and family sit and chat along with two television sets to keep everyone entertained. You will also be shocked to know that the motor home doesn't have two or three but a total of ten bunk beds so that is plenty of space to house quite a few people without much effort.

Vin Diesel’s Stylish Motor Home

Vin Diesel has made his name in the world of action movies and is a favorite actor for a lot of people all over the world. This Fast And Furious star is also the owner of an impressive motor home. It is a two-story, huge, luxurious, 1100 square foot motor bus that is worth almost $1.1 million.

It has a comfortable and big living room where Vin Diesel likes to relax in his free time. This is a spacious and hi-tech motor home that is equipped with 3D flat screen television sets and surveillance cameras for keeping an eye around the trailer for safety purposes. Downstairs there is a media lounge and a small portion is designed for the office work while the upstairs is the play area for kids. Another interesting feature is the mirror windows that are placed along the length of motor home for the good view.

Simon Cowell’s X-Factor Bus

The next motor home to makes it on our list is a 1000 square foot, 40 - ton and a double storey huge palace that can be seen moving on the roads near X-Factor venues has been customized for Simon Cowell. There is a long list of exceptional features that this amazing motor home has, starting with the living area.

It has an Italian leather interior, a 60 plus inch 3D TV, windows with electric shades that are engineered with solar tinted glass and argon filled thermal units to lessen the load of the A/C and there is also a small bar area. Upstairs there is a huge area for other events, including a makeup room and a fully furnished kitchen with a sub-zero refrigerator which is a must for any expensive motor home.

The Eli Young Band Motor Home

The band Eli Young  also owns an expensive motor home that not only acts as their residence on the go but also as their tour bus. This is a 52 square-foot amazing motor home that has been customized according to their tastes. It has been designed in a way that it can easily accommodate all the members of the band at any given time.

There is quite a big living room area with an entertainment corner and the interior is also very stylish. It has 8 beds that are placed one above the other making two rows of four beds and it also has a back lounge that is double the size of a jam studio. While going for a performance, the band enjoys their ride on the motor home by playing some music, relaxing and enjoying some chit chat.

Kellie Pickler’s Faye

Here is the customized and elegant tour bus of the famous singing sensation Kellie Pickler. She has named her bus as ‘Faye’ after her grandmother’s name. It has a spacious living room with beautiful white sofas and there are also some family photographs placed in one of the corners.

Some of the other rooms in this motor home include a kitchen, a bedroom, a kennel for her dogs, and an ice machine. The motor bus has four TVs and there are compact guest rooms as well as a guest bathroom. The main highlight of this motor home is the customized space for the most important things belonging to Kellie, as she loves to buy shoes from wherever she goes so there is a beautiful closet in which there are a whopping 42 pairs of shoes. Another amazing feature of this motor home is the steamer where Kellie spends her free time.

Will Smith’s Road Beast

Will Smith's motor home is worth $2 million, it's huge and is one of the most expensive and luxurious motor homes out there. This trailer has two levels and it occupies a space of 1200 square feet. It is nothing less than a huge mansion as the sides open, transforming it into a double decker motor home. It is a dream motor home.

It has a state of the art living room, a screening room, a make up room with automatic sliding door, a dining room with a kitchen and a makeup station. Next to the kitchen is the wardrobe area and also a private lounge. On board there is also a unique bathroom, the construction cost of which has been estimated to be almost $25,000 since it has a steam shower and the glass doors that make the bathroom opaque. The second level is the mind boggling screening room that can accommodate 30 individuals and it is equipped with some really cool features.

Mariah Carey’s Fun Motor Home

Mariah Carey, the world famous pop music star owns a double space trailer. This motor home can become a two storey bus by just pressing a button and has many mind blowing features. It consists of a comfortable yet stylish living room, a makeup area which has real makeup lights making it easier for her to do makeup and the best thing is that it has its own upstairs dance club that can accommodate thirty people, which goes on to show that Mariah definitely loves to party.

The first level living area is a whopping 600 square feet as the sides slip outward making it more wide. On the other hand, it has also been equipped with a really good sound system and a 65 inch television which is more than enough for Mariah's entertainment needs. The kitchen of this vehicle is also quite amazing and it comes equipped with a microwave, refrigerator, stove, a water machine and it is very spacious as well.

Hanson Brothers Classy Motor Home

The Hanson Brothers truly know how to travel with style. They own a 52 foot luxury tour bus that has been designed with perfection. This customized vehicle has Italian wood cabinetry and the couches and ceiling is of high quality leather. By the press of just a single button, this tour bus can transform into an impressive apartment.

While driving, it doesn’t occupy more space on the road and whenever you need it to, it expands making itself more spacious. It has all the facilities that a normal home would have. The living room seems to be a cigar lounge with brown and black color scheming. Being musicians, they surely need some good music on the go so it has a state of the art sound system with the ceiling housing the speakers and the floor housing the sub woofers. It also has a customized small kitchen for the crew to enjoy meals in while they are on the move.

Star Motor Home Of Lady Antebellum

This motor home provides the trio with all the helpful things that they need while traveling or on tour. It has a comfortable living room that features two leather couches where the group usually relaxes after doing a show, gossip and enjoy music. It also has a small stylish kitchen which includes a microwave and a refrigerator.

The interior of this motor home shows exactly how much hard work has been done in designing it and the whole theme of it has been created around the idea of musical instruments. Even the outside of this motor home is impressive and it attracts the attention of the bystanders since it also features a large picture of these three, making it quite unique and personalized co pared to other motor homes out there. The sides of this motor home also expand providing more space if required.

The Extraordinary Tour Bus Of Gretchen Wilson

Now it is the turn of the famous Gretchen Wilson motor home. Wilson actually has a sort of phobia associated to traveling in an airplane which is why she was willing to spend a good amount of cash on a motor home which she now uses to do most of her traveling on.

This motor home is a classic both from the inside as well as the outside. There is a black color scheme all around it and Wilson can usually be found inside this motor home before and after any of her shows. As far as amenities are concerned then it features a comfortable lounge with a television and a really good music system. The kitchen is also quite large as compared to other motor homes since it has a large refrigerator, microwave and all the other kitchen items that one would usually need. The interior color scheming and decorations of this motor home are not very feminine but still go on to show that a girl lives here.

Miranda Lambert’s Airstream

Miranda Lambert is the proud owner of one of the most popular and classic styles of motor home, the Airstream. This particular one has been re-designed recently for her. It’s a very reliable motor home which takes her to different places. She has spared some space for her guests as well in this luxury travel home.

She has bunk beds for her friends and family who travel with her or sometimes come for some chit chat. There is also some space for her dresses and shoes which she needs during traveling and a nice pink color refrigerator where she puts her snacks, wine and water. It also has a makeup room where she does her makeup and it has drawers and cupboards so that she can place her products there. There is a bedroom next to the makeup corner which features a large cozy bed where she can relax. Her dogs also travel with her so there is also space for them too.

The Impressive Motor Home Of Chad Kroeger

We're pretty sure most of you already know about Chad Kroeger especially since Nickelback gets a lot of hate on the internet some times. Still, that hasn't stopped Kroeger from making a ton of money from the music that he has released. He owns one of the biggest and most expensive motor homes out there. The interior of it is designed in such a way which totally depicts that it is owned by a rock star.

The feature that makes this motor home really stand out is that the interior of it is lined up with ostrich leather which if you didn't know cost almost four times as more as normal leather. A small kitchen is also there with a microwave oven, coffee machine and a few other kitchen related products. There is a small dining table in order to make more space for guests. In the living there is a customized long couch with a drawer to keep things safe, two bunk beds with personal TVs and also a couple of master beds. Surprisingly, it also has a king size bed, a large shower room and a mirror window to see whatever is going on outside.

Barack Obama’s Secure Motor Home

What would one expect from the motor home that was used to carry around the American president? Well for starters, one can expect it to have a lot of security, way more than your average celebrity motor home. As you can see in the picture above, all the windows on this vehicle are tinted, for security reasons obviously and a large number of secret service agents are always out and about, making sure that nothing is out of the ordinary.

Then comes the fact that is has been armored to the teeth, making it both bullet and bomb proof. Inside there are all the usual amenities that one would expect from a motor home but also critical communication equipment that allows the president to stay in touch with his advisors and anyone else regardless of where he might be. The cost for this amazing vehicle you ask? Well, it is estimated to be somewhere around the $1.1 million figure. It is mostly used when the president needs to be out and about during campaign season since the official presidential limo with a motorcade can be costly.

John Mayer’s Beastly Home On Wheels

The last celebrity on our list is the famous song writer, John Mayer. This young talented guy is behind no one in showing off his wealth. He owns a $500,000 worth of Earth XV-LTS Roamer motor home. This has to be one of the most unique motor homes on this list and the proof of that is in the picture above. This motor home is not only beautiful from the inside but it is meant to go to all those places where an average motor home shouldn't.

It has a powerful engine with a power stroke V8 which produces a massive 300 horsepower and 600 lb ft of torque making it possible to ride it on pretty much any terrain out there. With no compromise on comfort, this motor home is customized with a king size bed while the kitchen is decked out with basic amenities like an espresso machine and even a spice rack with a stove and a sink. This motor home is owned only by some of the richest people in the world and it gives off a message which is exactly what someone like Mayer wants.
