It's exciting to see Ariana Grande continue to make her music dreams come true by starring as Glinda in the movie adaptation of the popular musical Wicked. But even though this part is a career milestone for the young star, all eyes are on her dating life. Fans are paying a lot of attention to Grande's alleged complicated relationship with Ethan Slater and her divorce from Dalton Gomez.

As of October 2023, Dalton Gomez and Ariana Grande's divorce is final. While the divorce settlement has the usual financial information about property and a prenup, there is another part that stands out. Here's why Gomez and Grande aren't allowed to write about one another or share pictures of their marriage.

What Does Dalton Gomez And Ariana Grande's Divorce Settlement Say?

From the very beginning of their relationship Dalton Gomez and Ariana Grande were always as private as they could be. While the singer definitely knows that she has a massive platform, she doesn't always want people to know everything about her life. Of course, since she has been famous for so many years, people are curious.

Even when the couple got married in May 2021, fans didn't find out a lot about the big day. People reported that it was a "tiny and intimate" event. But unlike other celebrities who give lengthy interviews about their dresses and hairstyles and decor choices, Grande kept this big moment to herself.

But now that the couple is officially divorced, Gomez and Grande's divorce settlement has been shared publicly. It includes something that isn't typically part of divorce settlements, whether a couple is famous or not.

According to The Daily Mail, The Blast received the settlement, which says that Gomez isn't allowed to write about his marriage to the singer. He also can't post photos of her or them together.

According to the divorce settlement, he isn't able to "give any interview, write, appear in connection with, or assist or cooperate in the preparation or presentation of any book, article, interview, program or other production or publication of any kind whatsoever concerning the other party."

Gomez also can't share "any photograph (and related negatives), tape, film or like embodiments in all forms whether now or hereafter created or other record or recording of any aspect of any activity in or about any home, office or other property owned."

Why Can't Dalton Gomez Or Ariana Grande Share Images Or Stories About Their Marriage?

According to The Daily Mail, there is an explanation for why Ariana Grande and Dalton Gomez's divorce settlement includes this rule.

The singer has always seemed to want to stay as low-key as she can, and now that she is single again, she doesn't want her ex-husband to talk about her.

The documents say that after signing this agreement, Gomez "acknowledges that (Grande's) privacy and the non-disclosure of confidential information regarding him are vitally important."

Grande might also want to set a precedent for her future relationships. If she does get married again, it's possible that, if things ever went south, she would include the same rule in that divorce settlement.

While no one can say for sure, it seems likely that the former pair talked about this rule beforehand. Gomez must be completely fine with it.

Following his separation from Ariana Grande, Dalton Gomez reportedly has been going through a lot of changes.

Ariana Grande has been in several very public relationships. When she was dating and then engaged to Pete Davidson, people followed her every move and were amazed that the couple committed to each other so fast.

While fans wonder what happened between Grande and Gomez, they seem to want to be able to move on cleanly and freely without worrying about the other person talking about the marriage.

It makes sense that the singer wouldn't want her ex-husband to share anything about her. She doesn't seem to want to talk about him, either, which feels respectful.

The divorce settlement rule seems logical considering that Grande decided that she didn't want photos of her former husband on her Instagram account. According to People, she deleted wedding pictures from her Instagram account in July 2023.

While Ariana Grande's dating life is definitely pretty dramatic at times, she is always polite and respectful when the relationships end. She doesn't seem to have said anything mean about Pete Davidson or any of her other exes.

While some might say that the divorce settlement rule sounds a little harsh, it does seem like it will prevent Grande and Gomez from getting into any public fights or disagreements in the future.

Even before their divorce settlement was made public, Ariana Grande and Dalton Gomez didn't talk about their relationship or marriage. This seems to be based on a choice that the singer made several years ago when observing Zach Braff and Florence Pugh's romance.

In 2020, when Pugh explained to her fans that she didn't want to talk about the relationship, Grande commented on Pugh's post and agreed with everything that she said.

According to People, Grande said, "Beautifully worded and deeply appreciated. Sharing special, personal life things that make u happy on the internet can be truly traumatic. I know I’ve taken a step back from doing so to protect my loved ones and myself but I just wanted to share this and let u know how perfectly u expressed this and how appreciated u are for doing so.”

While Gomez hasn't talked about Grande's celebrity presence in interviews, sources have explained how he reportedly felt about it.

According to People, a source said in 2021, “Dalton’s entirely unfazed by the scope of her celebrity and is very grounding for her. He’s young but mature and knows what he wants out of life." However, in July 2023, TMZ reported that Gomez had trouble with the level of fame that Grande has and that contributed to their divorce.
