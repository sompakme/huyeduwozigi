Brett Stewart is currently dating American actor Daniel Durant.

Durant is known for starring as Moritz Stiefel in the 2015 Broadway revival of Spring Awakening, and CODA (2021).

The award-winning actor also played a recurring role in the ABC Family series Switched at Birth.

About Britt Stewart

Britt Stewart began dancing at age 3, when she enrolled at the Artistic Fusion Dance Academy.

The Colorado native was shot to fame when she appeared as a troupe member on Dancing with the Stars from season 23 to season 27.

Stewart became the first black female pro on the show in its 29th season, after she paired with Olympic figure skater Johnny Weir.

Other stars she has partnered with on the show include actor Martin Kove, and actor Daniel Durant.

Aside from Dancing with the Stars, Stewart has also embarked on dance tours for A-list artistes such as Annie Lennox, Demi Lovato, Florence and the Machine, Janet Jackson, Rihanna, Selena Gomez, and Katy Perry.

Stewart has subsequently appeared in all three High School Musical movies, and performed in the shows Bunheads, Gilmore Girls, Grey’s Anatomy, and The Voice.

Stewart has also danced at Super Bowl XLIX and on the ABC Family show Dancing Fools.
