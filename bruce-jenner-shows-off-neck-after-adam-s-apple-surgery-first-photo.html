Nice neck! Bruce Jenner was photographed for the first time on Monday, Feb. 10, showing off his bare neck after having surgery on his Adam's apple. Click here for more pics of Bruce Jenner.

The 64-year-old estranged husband of Kris Jenner stepped out in Thousand Oaks, Calif. while filming scenes for Keeping Up With the Kardashians. Jenner, sporting long hair, wore a blue T-shirt that exposed his neck during the outing.

The E! star recently underwent a procedure at Beverly Hills Surgical Center to flatten his Adam's apple. A source tells Us Weekly the Olympian intended to have the work done in December but postponed it until Jan. 19 after his plans were leaked.
