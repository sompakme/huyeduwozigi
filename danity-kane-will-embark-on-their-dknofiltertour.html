Danity Kane Will Embark On Their #DKNoFilterTour

 Posted on July 3, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  350 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

The four standing ladies of Danity Kane are taking their show on the road. Aubrey, Dawn, Andrea and Shannon will kick off their social media friendly #DKNoFilterTour in San Francisco on May 16 and conclude in San Diego on June 5. To promote the transparency of the national tour, the ladies used unfiltered selfies of over 100,000 fans to create a mosaic in their likeness. “To our fans: Danity Kane is doing this for you,” they said. “We want to give, see, and promote a real, unrestricted, uncensored, unfiltered life! That’s why we make music and that’s the way we want to see world! Our VIP tickets go on sale Wednesday and ALL tickets are on sale Friday at Livenation.com for you all to support our movement.”
