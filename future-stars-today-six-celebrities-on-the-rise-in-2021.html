The entertainment industry has bred another set of fresh faces that are climbing the ladder of fame amidst the pandemic. Film shootings were interrupted by the social distancing and isolation norms that became necessary to cut the spread of the COVID-19; however, once the condition was slightly under control, the actors returned to their jobs of entertaining the masses. A horde of new movies and TV shows has been released in one year, and several new celebrities have stolen the spotlight. The fame and attention they have grabbed have been distinct because the audience has sought refuge in these tough times by following up on these new productions.

Here are some of the celebrities that are on the rise in 2021.

Odessa Young

Odessa is a 23-year-old Australian actress who made it big in the film industry with her phenomenal performances. Amongst her most notable works are the feature films released in 2015 called Looking for Grace and The Daughter.

The Daughter even received critical acclaim, and Young bagged an AACTA Award for 'Best Actress' in a Leading Role. Young's performance in the hallucinatory thriller by Josephine Decker called Shirley, released in 2020, was one of her latest impressive roles co-starring with Elizabeth Moss. She also signed up for some other projects like the miniseries based on Stepenhe's Famous novel The Stand. Her net worth in 2021 is estimated to be about $1 million.

Archie Renaux

Archie Renaux was born Archie James Beale in Kingston, Greater London, England. The stunning 23-year-old has had a prosperous career in modeling and acting. The latest reason for his rising fame in pop culture is his brilliant performance in the Netflix show called Shadow Bone, where he plays one of the main characters Malyen 'Mal' Oretsev.

Renaux has also performed in other remarkable projects like the 2019 movie Gold Digger. Among his other recent projects are 2021's Voyagers and 2022's Morbius. In 2021, Archie is estimated to have a net worth of approximately $1 million.

Maitreyi Ramakrishnan

Maitreyi Ramakrishnan took the internet by storm after playing a role in the Netflix teen drama show called Never Have I Ever. The 19-year-old actress of Tamil descent was born on 28 December in Mississauga, Canada.

The glory of this teen actress is in the fact that she beat about 15,000 contestants to land the role of Devi Vishwakumar in the teen comedy-drama series. This role changed her life and career forever. She also became one of the Plan International Canada organization ambassadors that advocates child rights and gender equality. In 2021, Maitreyi has a net worth of about $100,000.

Tiffany Boone

Tiffany Boone was born in 1987 in Baltimore, Maryland, and started gaining prominence in the movie industry in the latter half of the 2010s. Before that, she had already performed in the gothic romance called Beautiful Creatures that came out in 2013 and in the crime series The Following that aired from 2013 to 2015.

She stood out with outstanding performances in George Clooney's Netflix sci-fi Drama called The Midnight Stars, the drama show on Amazon Prime called Little Forest Everywhere, and most importantly, another show called Hunters where she shared the screen with Al Pacino. Boone has been very grateful for having landed such great roles along with famous senior stars, and she has been particularly excited about her role in the miniseries Nine Perfect Strangers, also starring Nicole Kidman and Melissa McCarthy. She has a net worth of over $1 million in 2021.

Han Ye-Ri

Han Ye-Ri is a South Korean actress who has been a staple talent in her own country for a while. However, lately, she has made it big in the mainstream entertainment industry as well. One of her first performances that received favorable critical reception was Worst Woman, a work by Kim Jon-Kwan that came out in 2016.

Following this, she has performed in other shows such as Hello, My Twenties, Nodku Flower, and My Unfamiliar Family. She garnered even more fame by her performance as Monica Yi opposite Steve Yeun in the award-winning film Minari by Lee Isaac Chung. The net worth of the talented actress is not exactly known, but she is rumored to have a million-dollar fortune.

John David Washington

John David Washington was born on July 28 in 1984, in Los Angeles, California, to Denzel Washington and Pauletta Washington. Thus, the family influence in helping young John David develop acting skills was quite strong.

He made his name first as a football player. It was later that he became a part of the Hollywood industry. Washington made a grand entry into Hollywood through the movie Blackklansman and Christopher Nolan's famous Tenet. One of his latest phenomenal performances has been in the Netflix original called Malcolm and Marie, where he starred opposite the talented Zendaya. John presently has an impressive net worth of about $8 million.

Looking at these rising stars, it seems very believable that the film industry may never stop blooming and producing new talents no matter how dark times get.
