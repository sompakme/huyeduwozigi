How Much Did Kelsey Grammer Pay Camille Meyer?

 Posted on August 19, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  617 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Camille Meyer first came to prominence following her marriage to Frasier actor, Kelsey Grammer back in 1997. Despite their years of marriage together and two children, the couple found themselves divorcing following Camille's very first season on the Real Housewives.

Camille appeared on the first two seasons of RHOBH alongside Kyle Richards and Lisa Vanderpump, before taking on a guest role for the remainder of the series.

While her life appeared to be picture-perfect, it all came tumbling down when reports revealed that Kelsey filed for divorce. Considering the couple did not have a prenup, and the actor was worth a whopping $80 million, fans were immediately wondering how much Camille would walk away with.

Kelsey Grammer's Divorce From Camille Meyer

After shocking fans with her luxurious lifestyle come the first season of the Real Housewives Of Beverly Hills, Camille Grammer, now Meyer's life was nothing close to perfect.

Camille signed onto the show after original cast member, Kyle Richards, brought her on alongside Camille's husband, esteemed actor, Kelsey Grammer. The cameras then began filming the very first season, where Camille's life would be caught crashing down.

Come to the end of RHOBH's first-go, Camille and Kelsey's marriage came to a shocking end, however, it appears as if the only one who was shocked, was Camille.

The star, who had four nannies at the time, was labeled at the season one reunion as the "most hated housewife of all time", however, fans had no idea what Camille was going through at the time.

It was then that Camille revealed Kelsey Grammer no longer wanted to be with her following their 10 years of marriage. Camille was reportedly "blindsided" and "devastated" over the news.

How Much Was Thd Divorce Settlement?

When news broke that Kelsey and Camille Grammer would be no more, one question that immediately came to mind was whether or not the couple had a prenup.

Well, considering they didn't, it was clear that Camille would be walking away with quite the divorce settlement. Following the finalization of their divorce in 2011, it was revealed that Camille walked away with a $30 million settlement, which has since been estimated to be worth a whopping $42 million today!

This was one of the biggest divorce settlements seen in Housewives history, and Camille felt as if she earned every penny! "I just didn't sit back, buying fancy clothes and shoving bonbons in my face. I mean, I worked hard," she said.

During Kelsey Grammer's interview with Oprah Winfrey, the Frasier actor revealed that he definitely had some remorse regarding his handling of their split. The actor shared that he knew their marriage was over before even signing on for Housewives.

Kelsey claims he felt as if his wife never received the fame she always wanted, and supporting her decision to go on the show was his way of ensuring she'd get what she always wanted, as he explained.

Who Is Camille Married To Now?

Luckily for Camille, her divorce didn't stop her from finding love! The former RHOBH star began dating David C. Meyer following her time on the show, and the duo was officially engaged come 2017.

After returning to the series in a guest role, Camille's wedding was documented by Bravo cameras for the show. Fellow co-star, Kyle Richards was actually a bridesmaid in Camille's second wedding, while Lisa Rinna, Dorit Kemsley, and Teddi Mellencamp were also in attendance.

The couple tied the knot in a stunning Hawaiian ceremony, and have remained happily together ever since!
