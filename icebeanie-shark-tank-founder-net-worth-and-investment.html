What Is IceBeanie?

IceBeanie is an all-natural compression cold therapy hat designed to provide relief from headaches, concussions, and migraines. Made of soft microfiber polyester, this cold therapy hat is a convenient and effective solution for natural headache and migraine relief. The hat features acupressure-designed gel packs that can be cooled in the freezer for 45 minutes before use, delivering targeted cold therapy to the head. With its stretchable fabric, IceBeanie fits comfortably on any shaped head, allowing users to easily adjust the level of compression to their preference.

IceBeanie’s cold compression therapy has been shown to reduce inflammation and promote relaxation, making it an ideal choice for individuals seeking drug-free headache relief. By providing a gentle, cooling sensation, IceBeanie can help alleviate pain and discomfort associated with headaches and migraines. This natural compression cold therapy can also be used as a preventative measure for athletes or individuals recovering from concussions.

IceBeanie offers a convenient and user-friendly solution for those in need of headache relief. With its innovative design and high-quality materials, this cold therapy hat provides effective relief without the use of drugs or medication. Whether you’re experiencing a tension headache or seeking relief after a sports injury, IceBeanie can help provide the relief you need in a natural and safe way.

IceBeanie Shark Tank Update

IceBeanie had an exciting opportunity to showcase their innovative product on Season 12 Episode 13 of Shark Tank. Founder Nic Lamb presented the benefits of IceBeanie to the esteemed panel of Sharks, including billionaire entrepreneur Mark Cuban.

“IceBeanie provides relief from migraines and headaches, using natural compression cold therapy. It’s a game-changer for those seeking a drug-free solution to alleviate their symptoms,”

The episode featuring IceBeanie on Shark Tank aired on February 12, 2021, captivating audiences with its innovative solution and entrepreneurial journey.

Lamb endured the challenges of post-concussion migraines following a severe head injury sustained while surfing. Seeking relief from the debilitating migraines, Lamb discovered the benefits of cold compression therapy.

This personal journey sparked his determination to find a solution for others going through similar experiences. With a vision to provide relief and comfort to individuals suffering from migraines and concussions, Lamb channeled his inspiration into the development of IceBeanie.

IceBeanie is a unique cold therapy hat designed to alleviate headaches, migraines, and the symptoms of head injuries. This innovative product utilizes natural compression therapy to provide targeted cooling relief. The incorporation of acupressure-designed gel packs, which can be cooled in the freezer, enhances the therapeutic effects of the hat.

The revelation of the positive impact of cold compression therapy on his own recovery motivated Lamb to create IceBeanie, ensuring that individuals facing similar challenges would have access to a convenient and effective solution.

Did IceBeanie Get a Deal on Shark Tank?
