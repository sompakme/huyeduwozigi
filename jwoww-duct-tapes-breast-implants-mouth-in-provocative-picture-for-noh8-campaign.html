JWoww Duct-Tapes Breast Implants, Mouth in Provocative Picture for NOH8 Campaign

 Posted on July 20, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  200 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

No shirt, no h8, no problem. Jenni "JWoww" Farley recently lent her face (and other assets) to the NOH8 Campaign for marriage, gender, and human equality — and the result is indeed "Woww"-worthy. If her provocative photos don't draw some attention to the cause, nothing will.

The 27-year-old Jersey Shore alum put her own sexy spin on photographer Adam Bouska's iconic pictures, which typically feature subjects with duct tape over their mouths and the "NOH8" symbol painted on their cheeks. (The duct tape symbolizes people's voices being silenced by Proposition 8, an amendment to the California state constitution that bans same-sex marriage.)

JWoww's version of the concept features the usual duct-taped mouth and "NOH8" symbol, plus a makeshift duct-tape bra that covers (albeit barely) the MTV star's infamous breast implants. In one striking shot, the Snooki & JWoww standout — engaged to Roger Mathews — poses in just the bra, black pants, and a white T-shirt that has been ripped open to expose her tattooed torso.
