Kylie Jenner Sparks Engagement Rumors As She Wears Diamond While Hanging With Timothee Chalamet

 Posted on August 5, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  523 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Highlights

  • Kylie Jenner and Timothee Chalamet's relationship is sparking engagement rumors after Kylie was seen wearing a suspiciously large diamond ring on her left hand.
  • The couple has been seen on multiple occasions, engaging in PDA and coordinating their outfits, indicating that their relationship is becoming more serious.
  • While Kylie and Travis Scott continue to co-parent amicably, there are reports that Travis is finding it difficult to see his ex move on with Timothee. However, Travis has not publicly commented on their relationship.

Kylie Jenner and Timothee Chalamet’s relationship appears to be heating up. So much so, the new couple have become the subject of engagement rumors after Kylie was spotted wearing a diamond on her left ring finger.

In new photos published by Daily Mail, Timothee and Kylie were seen entering Folderol Wine Bar in Paris to celebrate singer Rosalía's birthday. However, what instantly captured the fans' curiosity was the suspiciously large ring on Kylie’s left hands.

The couple kept close in coordinated outfits. Kylie wore black belted trend coat, high-heels boots, and sunglasses, while Timothee was dressed in a puffy bomber jacket, blue jeans, and a baseball cap covering most of his face.

For now, Kylie hasn’t commented on the speculation over whether it was an engagement ring or not. But this isn’t the first time the makeup mogul has worn what seemed like an engagement ring, just to spark a lot of speculation.

In November 2021, Travis Scott gifted Kylie a diamond ring when she was expecting their second child. He also got a matching albeit smaller version for their daughter, Stormi. Many fans assumed it was an engagement ring given the size of the stone, even though she wasn’t wearing it on her left ring finger when she showcased it on social media.

Ultimately, however, Kylie and Travis never announced an engagement. They broke up the following year.

Kylie Jenner Sparks Engagement Rumors to Timothee Chalamet 

Kylie and Timothee made their relationship debut in public in early September, after months of dating speculation. They’ve been seen on numerous occasions getting PDA-heavy. Initially, reports said the pair were taking things slow and keeping it casual. "They are keeping things casual at this point," an insider told Entertainment Tonight in August. "It's not serious, but Kylie is enjoying hanging out with Timothée and seeing where it goes.”

“It's been really fun for her because it feels a lot different than her past relationships,” they continued. “It's new and exciting for Kylie and she's having a lot of fun."

However, the couple’s recent public sightings – and Kylie’s decision to wear a ring on that finger, knowing it could spark speculation – seems to suggest things are moving in a more serious direction with the two.

While Kylie and Travis maintain a publicly amicable co-parenting dynamic, there have been reports that the rapper- who’s facing billions in lawsuits over the fatal Astroworld tragedy – is having a hard time seeing his ex move on. However, Travis hasn’t publicly spoken about Kylie and Timothee’s blossoming romance.
