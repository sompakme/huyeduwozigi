Lala And Carmelo Anthony Reunite At Halloween NYC Party

 Posted on August 29, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  192 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Celebrity Halloween parties are always one to remember since they remind us that we should hire glam squads for the season and that celebs have super relatable personalities. Case in point, Lala Anthony and Lenny S’ remarkable Halloween bashes. Over the years the two have encouraged their friends to do more than the most for Halloween. This year was the same as the Maino, Elle Varner, Young Ma and many more arrived in their holiday best.

Going off at Vandal Wednesday (Oct. 31), the hottest in hip-hop and beyond took creative risks with their costumes.

Check them out below.
