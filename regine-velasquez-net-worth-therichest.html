Filipina recording artist and actress, Regina Velazquez has an estimated net worth of $14 million.

Born in the Philippines in 1970, Velazquez skyrocketed to Asian fame in 1989 after placing 1st at an annually held singing contest in Hong Kong. In 1994, Velazquez released her most popular album Listen Without Prejudice that sold more than 700,000 copies in Asia. While Velazquez does not have the US appeal of other international artists, she has had the honor of being the inaugural artist from Asia to perform as a solo act at Carnegie Hall in New York. Additionally, Velazquez has worked with many other artists like Brian McKnight, Mandy Moore, and 98 Degrees. Because of her musical success, Velasquez has been hailed as the Philippines’ best selling artist of all time with more than 7 million albums sold in the country and an additional 500,000 sold in Asia. When not performing, Velazquez has had a successful role in several movies and television shows and operates her own record label company called IndiMusic.
