Beyonce has a reported net worth of $500 million, while her husband Jay-Z is a certified billionaire. And with her status as a fashion icon, it’s no surprise that Beyonce has rocked some very expensive clothing and jewelry pieces throughout the years.

Below, we take a look into the priciest things the singer has ever put on her body, with some outfits alone costing millions.

One Of Beyonce’s Instagram Look Cost Over $20,000

Beyonce isn’t shy to post glamorous photos for her millions of social media followers, and there’s apparently a lot of money that goes into getting her iconic shots. The singer’s outfits alone often cost a small fortune.

In 2017, the former Destiny’s Child member made headlines for making an Instagram post featuring $20,000 in clothing.

The slideshow featured images of Beyonce and Jay-Z on vacation, even some on their private plane. One picture showed Queen B in Saint Laurent over-the-knee boots, worth over $3,500, which was paired with a $2,000 Gucci denim jacket.

In a different picture, Beyonce could be seen wearing bedazzled boots, also from Saint Laurent, valued at over $10,000.

Given that they spend tens of thousands of dollars on their vacation outfits, we can’t imagine how much their accommodation and entertainment costs are when traveling.

Beyonce’s Spent At Least $50,000 On Her Maternity Wardrobe

When expecting pregnant with her twins in 2017, Beyonce reportedly spent at least $50,000 on her maternity wardrobe. Arguably, the most iconic look she wore at the time was the gold, Grecian-inspired dress and gown, which she performed in at the 2017 Grammy’s.

Beyonce’s Vow Renewal Dress Cost $70,000

In 2018, Beyonce and Jay-Z renewed their vows in honor of their 10th wedding anniversary, and the singer wore a stunning gown inspired by Queen Victoria’s wedding dress. The custom design reportedly cost $70,000.

Beyonce’s BET Bodysuit Featured 30,000 Swarovski Crystals

Beyonce stunned at the 2016 BET awards when she performed in a lavish bodysuit that featured 30,000 individual Swarovski Crystals. The piece reportedly took 3 weeks to make by hand and was designed by Julien Macdonald.

It’s not known how much the bodysuit cost, but given the details and amount of crystals need, it’s likely worth tens of thousands, if not more.

Beyonce’s Grammy Dress Later Sold For $250,000

For the 2014 Grammy Awards, Beyonce dazzled in a stunning white Michael Costello gown, valued at $20,000. But having a dress worn by Beyonce apparently increases its overall worth, as a superfan later offered to buy the dress for $250,000.

Beyonce Has A Collection Of Birkin Bags

Birkin bags are one of the most coveted purses on the planet, with people needing a direct invitation from the designer to purchase, unless they score one second-hand.

Beyonce reportedly has a collection of Birkins, and we’re guessing they weren’t pre-loved. The singer doesn’t have just one or two, either, but instead has a whole collection. Jay-Z reportedly surprised her a series of Birkin bags at once, valued at over $350,000.

It's unclear how much Beyonce’s entire purse collection is worth, but her taste for designer labels suggests that it could easily be worth millions.

She Wore $7.5 Million In Jewelry For A Dubai Performance

Earlier this year, Beyonce made a controversial performance in Dubai to celebrate the opening of a new hotel, Atlantis the Royal. She apparently made a huge paycheck for the concert -- $24 million. The hotel also put her up in their most luxurious penthouse, which costs a cool $100,000 per night.

However, the luxuries didn’t stop there. For the performance itself, Beyonce reportedly wore $7.5 million of Lorraine Schwartz jewelry.

The singer had multiple costume changes during the performance, and made sure her jewelry matched perfectly each time. She was seen wearing a pair of diamond and green Colombian emerald fan earrings, which features over 100-carats of diamonds. At the same time, she wore a 30-carat yellow diamond ring.

During another costume change, Beyonce wore teardrop 80-carat earrings plus a halo-inspired crown. Her final outfit for the concert was a bedazzled, pink dress made by Ukrianian designer Ivan Frolov. The matching gloves and stockings featured 60-carats of diamonds alone.

“The outfit was created in our workshop in Kyiv, Ukraine, during war and massive blackouts […] it just goes to show that no matter what, Ukrainian brands continue to showcase the world their resistance and culture,” the designer told Vogue of the dress.

Beyonce Wore Tiffany & Co.’s Most Expensive Diamond Necklace

While hosting an Oscar’s afterparty at Chateau Marmont in Los Angeles, Beyonce shimmered in the most expensive diamond necklace ever created by Tiffany & Co.

Known as the Historic 1939 World's Fair Necklace, the piece features an 80-carat Empire Diamond. It’s worth over $30 million.

Needless to say, Beyonce has a taste for the finer things in life, especially when it comes to clothing and jewelry. And apparently, her eldest child, Blue Ivy, is taking after her, as the 11-year-old has also worn some equally bougie outfits. We guess it runs in the family!
