The White Lotus Season Two Cast Ranked By Net Worth

 Posted on September 6, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  888 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Mike White is the mastermind behind everyone's current obsession, The White Lotus. White has written such films as Chuck & Buck and School of Rock. His latest project has taken off, becoming one of the most-watched and talked about shows of the year. The Emmy-winning show has brought more success for White and given him much-deserved recognition.

It has also included several extremely talented cast members. Some are stars in their own right and some are on their way to becoming stars. The latest season had a truly impressive cast of characters who did an amazing job at bringing the show to life.

But which one of the second season's amazing cast has the highest net worth? Let's find out.

8 Adam DiMarco - $1 Million

Adam DiMarco plays the role of Dominic's son Albie. He is on a trip with his father and grandfather to learn about his family's heritage. Albie is a kind character who takes on the role of peacekeeper within his family. DiMarco has appeared in various television series including Charmed, Chilling Adventures of Sabrina, The Magicians, The Good Doctor and The Order. He has also appeared in Supernatural, Arctic Air, and had a starring role in the Disney Channel Original Movie Radio Rebel, where he played Gavin.

7 Jon Gries - $2 Million

Jon Gries plays Greg, Tanya's husband. Greg was introduced in the first season of the show. In the second season of the show, Greg isn't thrilled with the fact that Tanya has brought her assistant Portia on the trip, which was supposed to be their romantic getaway. Gries is an accomplished and successful actor who has appeared in Napoleon Dynamite, Seinfeld, Lost, The Pretender, Genius, Criminal Minds, Dream Corp LLC, and The Bridge. He was nominated for an Independent Spirit Award for his role as Uncle Rico in Napoleon Dynamite.

6 Theo James - $4 Million

Theo James plays Cameron Babcock, a businessman who is on holiday with his wife Daphne, his college roommate Ethan and Harper, Ethan's wife. He is very successful and from a wealthy family. James is best known for starring in the Divergent films. James also had roles in projects such as Underworld, How It Ends, Archive, The Time Traveler's Wife, The Inbetweeners Movie, Downton Abbey, The Benefactor and Mr Malcolm's List. James has received several Teen Choice Awards and a People's Choice Award.

5 Tom Hollander - $5 Million

Tom Hollander plays Quentin, an English expat who becomes friends with Tanya. He is on holiday with his friends and nephew Jack. Prior to The White Lotus, Hollander co-wrote and starred in Rev, a sitcom that won a BAFTA Award. Hollander is best known for appearing in About Time, Pirates of the Caribbean, In the Loop, Pride & Prejudice, Gosford Park and The King's Man. He also played lead roles in Doctor Thorne and The Night Manager. He even made an appearance in the film Bird Box.

4 Jennifer Coolidge - $6 Million

Jennifer Coolidge plays the role of Tanya McQuoid-Hunt. Tanya is very unstable woman who is traveling with her husband Greg and her assistant Portia. Tanya is very wealthy and met her husband during the show's first season in Hawaii. Coolidge has appeared in several hit films and television shows. She has appeared in American Pie, Legally Blonde, A Cinderella Story, Promising Young Woman and 2 Broke Girls. Coolidge is an Emmy-winning actress who has been a regular in Christopher Guest's mockumentary films, such as in Best in Show, For Your Consideration, A Mighty Wind and Mascots.

3 Aubrey Plaza - $8 Million

Aubrey Plaza plays Harper Spiller, a woman whose husband Ethan achieved success in his career, resulting in them becoming very wealthy. Plaza became famous with her role in the show Parks and Recreation, where she played April. The show was nominated for many Emmys over the years and has been recognized by the Critics' Choice Television Awards and GLAAD Media Awards. Since the show, she has had starring roles in projects such as Legion, Safety Not Guaranteed, Scott Pilgrim vs the World, Monsters University and Life After Beth.

2 F Murray Abraham - $10 Million

F Murray Abraham plays the role of Bert Di Grasso. His character is an elderly man visiting Sicily with his son Dominic and his grandson Albie. He is becoming frail, but still views himself as strong and functional. Abraham is an Academy Award-winning actor who starred in 1984's Amadeus as Antonio Salieri. He also had memorable roles in Scarface, All the President's Men, Star Trek: Insurrection, The Grand Budapest Hotel and Isle of Dogs.

1 Michael Imperioli - $20 Million

Michael Imperioli plays Dominic Di Grasso, the son of Bert. Dominic is a Hollywood producer hoping to get in touch with his ancestral roots, along with his son and father. Imperioli won an Emmy for his role in another HBO show. He played the role of Christopher Moltisanti in The Sopranos. He has made appearances in Goodfellas, Jungle Fever, Bad Boys, Shark Tale, The Lovely Bones, One Night in Miami, Project Blue Book and Lucifer.
