These Are The Richest Women In Hollywood As Of 2023

 Posted on September 1, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  869 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Hollywood is home to some of the richest women in the world. These uber-wealthy women have amassed vast fortunes through their dominance of cinema, television, music, and business. These women have achieved remarkable success in their respective careers and turned their fame and business acumen into significant wealth, solidifying their positions as some of the richest women in Hollywood.

As of 2023, the wealthiest women in Hollywood include multimedia mogul Oprah Winfrey, worth $3.5 billion; benefactor and actress Jami Gertz, worth $8 billion; heiress and performer Dina Merrill, worth $5 billion; and actresses-turned-entrepreneur Ashley Olsen; who is worth $500 million. Join us as we examine the eight wealthiest women in Hollywood, revealing how they have leveraged their celebrity to build multi-billion or multi-hundred million dollar multimedia empires.

8 Julia Roberts -$250 million

Julia Roberts has amassed an astonishing net worth of $250 million. She skyrocketed to fame after hits like Pretty Woman and Erin Brockovich, for which she won an Oscar. With her prolific 30+ year acting career starring in wildly successful films like Ocean's Eleven, Notting Hill, Eat Pray Love and more, she became one of the highest-paid actresses, eventually commanding over $20 million per movie. Between those mega salaries and back-end points on her many box office smashes, Roberts has accrued immense wealth. America's sweetheart has earned every penny through her decades-long career as one of Hollywood's most beloved leading ladies.

7 Jessica Alba -$390 million

Jessica Alba has amassed a massive $390 million net worth. She became well-known for acting in blockbusters like Sin City and Fantastic Four. Along with her work in movies and TV, Alba has made millions through sponsorship deals. One of America's richest independently wealthy women is Jessica Alba, thanks to her entertainment and company empire.

6 Victoria Principal -$400 million

A $400 million estimated net worth belongs to Victoria Principal. She rose to popularity from 1978 to 1987 by portraying Pamela Barnes Ewing on the popular TV show Dallas. Victoria started her production business after Dallas and developed the famous Principal Secret skincare brand. Over the years, she has both produced and acted in some made-for-TV films.

5 Reese Witherspoon $400 Million

Witherspoon is a famous American producer, actress, and entrepreneur in the entertainment industry. Reese Witherspoon has had a successful acting career spanning several decades. Witherspoon has appeared in numerous commercially successful films and television series. Her acting roles, including those in hit movies like Legally Blonde, Sweet Home Alabama, and Walk the Line have earned her substantial paychecks. Witherspoon co-founded Pacific Standard, a production company that later became Hello Sunshine. Through this company, she has produced various successful film projects.

4 Ashley Olsen -$500 million

Ashley has an estimated net worth of $500 million. Along with her twin sister Mary-Kate, Ashley rose to fame as a child actress on Full House in the 90s. The Olsen twins then built a lucrative brand, licensing their name to movies, magazines, clothing, fragrances, and more. In 2006, they established high-end fashion labels The Row, Elizabeth, and James. Ashley's wealth comes from their dual entertainment and fashion empire and savvy investments. Despite living out of the spotlight today, Ashley Olsen has quietly amassed half a billion-dollar fortune alongside her twin sister.

3 Oprah Winfrey -$3.5 billion

Oprah has an estimated net worth of $3.1 billion, and her OWN network empire is home to one of the wealthiest and most influential women: Oprah Winfrey. Her household name came courtesy of her role as host of The Oprah Winfrey Show. By utilizing her fame, the Oprah Winfrey Network, O Magazine, and her successful production company were established. On top of that, the shares she owns in Weight Watchers and her stake in Discovery are worth hundreds of millions of dollars.

2 Dina Merrill -$5 billion

Dina Merrill amassed a $5 billion fortune before her death in 2017. She was the heiress to the Post Cereals empire, having been born Nedenia Hutton. During her six decades-long acting career, Merrill featured in several films and television shows, frequently utilizing her fortune to fund productions herself. Along with acting, she and her husband Ted Hartley ventured into business. Their entertainment company RKO remains profitable today. Merrill amassed one of the greatest personal fortunes in Hollywood history, with an estimated $5 billion net worth, thanks to her inheritance and astute financial management.

1 Jami Gertz -$8 billion

Jami is an American actress and philanthropist known for her work in film and television. Jami's fame in 1980s movies/TV shows such as The Lost Boys and Square Pegs came while Gertz married billionaire Tony Ressler, co-founder of Ares Management. Ares has Gertz serving on their board, and he is also a philanthropist. Founded by her, the nonprofit Giving Back Fund is a stakeholder in the Atlanta Hawks. With Ares ties, Jami Gertz has emerged as one of the wealthiest women in entertainment, boasting an estimated fortune of $8 billion.
