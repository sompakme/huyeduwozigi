Tori Spelling Plans To Profit From Her New Boyfriend After Divorcing Dean McDermott

Highlights

  • Tori Spelling considers starring in a dating reality show to find love again.
  • Successful dating reality shows could pave the way for Spelling's TV journey.
  • Dean McDermott is already dating Lily Calo, who supports his sobriety.

After Tori Spelling and Dean McDermott were married for many years, the couple separated, and then she filed for divorce. Since their split, there has been a lot of focus on why Spelling and McDermott were together so long and how Jennifer Lopez got dragged into the divorce.

As it turns out, however, Spelling would like for people to start focusing on her dating life soon. The reason for that is that Spelling admitted that she is open to in cashing in on her dating life now that she is single again.

This article will reveal how Tori Spelling hopes to profit from becoming single again. Then, the article will look at what has been reported about Dean McDermott's dating life since his split from Spelling.

Tori Spelling Is Open To Starring In A Reality Show About Her Dating Life

When Beverly Hills, 90210 made Tori Spelling one of the most famous people from her generation, she was known as an actor. Over time, however, that began to change as her career began to focus on the reality shows Spelling has headlined.

In many cases, Spelling's reality shows focused on her relationship with her ex, Dean McDermott, and their kids. According to an April 2024 Daily Mail report, an insider told the outlet that Spelling was open to starring in a reality show again that is mostly about her. In this case, Spelling would be happy to consider starring in a dating reality show.

"Whether it is organic or if she is put on a blind date, Tori wants to find love again. She would also consider a TV show that would be based on getting her a new man."

In the modern television landscape, many dating reality shows have become extremely successful. Most notably, shows from the Bachelor Nation franchise have become a huge deal. On top of that, dating shows are huge on Netflix, including Love is Blind, The Ultimatum, Too Hot to Handle, Love on the Spectrum, and many more.

In theory, it is easy to imagine Spelling joining some of those shows as a form of stunt casting. Failing that, Spelling easily could be the main star of a reality show solely focused on her search for a new love. After all, Brett Michaels and Flavor Flav spent years doing that.

In either scenario, Spelling could make a lot of money from bringing her attempts at dating to television.

The reason for Spelling's readiness to move on, according to the insider, is the true timeline of things. The insider explains that even though Spelling only recently filed for divorce, her marriage to Dean McDermott has in reality been over for a long time.

"Though her marriage is over, it has been over for a long time. She is ready to open her heart up for another relationship."

It is important to note that according to the insider, Spelling isn't just looking to get involved with anyone. Instead, the insider explained that Spelling feels that she deserves to feel the thrill of truly being in love again.

"She feels that she deserves it and would love to experience being happy and in love again. To have that in her next stage of life after everything she has been through would be incredible."

While Spelling is looking to fall in love again, that doesn't mean that is the only thing she is thinking about. After all, Daily Mail's report also touched on the fact that Spelling has argued that she should get sole custody of her children.

In April 2024, Tori Spelling spoke to E! News about how her children are coping with her divorce from Dean McDermott. "The kids are doing well and Dean is doing well. We're all hanging in. We'll always be a family. Whether there's a title on it or not so, it's just time to move forward."

With that in mind, it makes sense that Spelling's insider stated that her kids matter most to her. Still, the insider claimed that Spelling wants to find the right person who can understand why her kids come first, and they'll have to deal with the spotlight if they date her.

"There is lots to deal with getting into a new relationship with Tori and any guy needs to know that she is a mom first. But anyone who can deal with Tori - she is not afraid to dive in head first if the person has the right intentions and wants to grow things into love. She is 50 now and she isn't about having a mid-life crisis and just having flings. She wants and expects rewarding relationships from this point on and the sooner the better."

Dean McDermott Is Already Dating Following His Split From Tori Spelling

Not long after Tori Spelling filed for divorce from Dean McDermott, it became clear that he had a new girlfriend. In April 2024, People published an article about who McDermott's girlfriend was, and she seemed like a fascinating person.

In October 2023, the media spotted Dean McDermott with his new girlfriend, Lily Calo. The duo were spotted holding hands while walking around in Los Angeles. Days later, McDermott and Calo were spotted kissing as they were at the Los Angeles International Airport.

Since the media first spotted Calo, fans have learned that she is a successful professional who has lived in multiple states. However, the thing that many observers cared about most about is how the people in McDermott's life were reacting to Calo.

During an episode of her podcast, "misSpelling", Spelling revealed that she likes Calo and is adjusting to her dating McDermott. "I like Lily a lot. It's not bad. It's just you know, it's different." People's article about Calo also revealed that she has been spotted spending time with McDermott and his kids multiple times.

Perhaps the reason why Spelling and her kids have accepted Calo so fast is because McDermott says she is a good influence. As People revealed, McDermott told Page Six that Calo has supported his fight to stay sober.

“She’s a great support for me … we check in with each other every day and keep each other accountable,” McDermott said. “She just really is a great — great help to me.”
