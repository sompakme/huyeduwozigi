What’s the difference between Largo and adagio?

Largo – slow and broad (40–60 bpm) … Adagio – slow with great expression (66–76 bpm) Adagietto – slower than andante (72–76 bpm) or slightly faster than adagio (70–80 bpm) Andante – at a walking pace (76–108 bpm)
