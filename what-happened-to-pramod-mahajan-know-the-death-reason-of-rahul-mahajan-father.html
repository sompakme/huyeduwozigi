Pravin Mahajan who was convicted for killing his brother, BJP leader Pramod Mahajan, has died in a Mumbai hospital. What Happened To Him?

Pramod Mahajan was an Indian politician from Maharashtra. A second-generation leader of the BJP, he belonged to a group of relatively young technocratic leaders.

He was a member of the Rajya Sabha and a General Secretary of his party. He contested only two Lok Sabha elections from the Mumbai-North East constituency. 

The politician took part in the agitation against the state of emergency imposed by Prime Minister Indira Gandhi and was incarcerated in the Nahsik central jail until it was lifted. Between 1983 and 1985, he was also the All India Secretary of his party.

Rahul Mahajan Father: Pramod Mahajan Death Reason 

Pravin Mahajan was convicted for killing his brother Pramod Mahajan, has died in a Mumbai hospital. He was shot by him over a family dispute. 

His brother is serving a life sentence in Nashik Jail, was admitted to Jupiter Hospital in Mumbai in December after a suspected brain hemorrhage. He was sentenced to life imprisonment by the court in 2007.

Pramod Mahajan Wife And Son Revealed

Pramod Mahajan was married to his beloved wife Rekha Hamine. His love for theatre brought him close to his partner.

Pramod Mahajan Family Details

Pramod Mahajan was born to Venkatesh Devidas Mahajan and Prabhavati Venkatesh Mahajan in Mahbubnagar, Telangana, India in a Deshastha Brahmin family.

The Mahajan family had migrated from their home in Mahajan gully in Osmanabad to Ambajogai and were staying in a rented house in Mangalwar Peth. He spent his childhood in Ambejogai. 

He attended Yogeshwari Vidyalaya and Mahavidyalaya in the Beed district of Maharashtra and studied at the Ranade Institute for Journalism, Pune. The politician obtained bachelor’s degrees in Physics and Journalism and a post-graduate degree in political science.
