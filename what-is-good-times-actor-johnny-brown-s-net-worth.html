Johnny Brown is an American actor and singer. Johnny Brown was born in St. Petersburg, Florida, in June 1937. He has performed on stage in addition to film and television. Brown was active from 1961 to 2013. He regularly appeared on Rowan & Martin’s Laugh-In from 1970 to 1972.

Brown had a diverse career ranging from recording songs and being a part of a music band to making an appearance on Broadway and being in a regular television series including “Rowan & Martin’s Laugh-In,” where he played as a part of the three seasons ensemble.

Brown became part of Good Times in 1975 when the former ‘Laugh-In’ writer turned producer introduced him in the middle of its second season.

Brown made his debut in a film in 1996 where he portrayed a blind pianist in a dramatical movie “A Man Called Adam.” He also starred in Man in the Mirror” with Martin Sheen in 2008.
