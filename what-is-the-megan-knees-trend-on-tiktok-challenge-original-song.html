What is The Megan Knees Trend on TikTok? Challenge &amp; Original Song

 Posted on July 4, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  398 words  |  Martina Birk

What is The Megan Knees Trend on TikTok? The viral trend has taken the internet by storm as many people are attempting the challenge. Stay with the article to find out more about it.

The Megan Knees Trend is the latest TikTok challenge where people see if they have the Megan Knees. The term, Megan Knees, means having strong legs as Megan Thee Stallion. Besides her music, she is known for her coordinated dances, leg strength, and twerking moves.

Many TikTokers are trying out the viral challenge, some have passed so well while some struggled. Some people have tried it on one knee, while some holding their children. TikTok users are also doing the Megan Knees Trend with their partners and friends, and getting millions of views.

The Megan Knees Trend on TikTok Explained

The viral Megan Knees Trend is making people test their leg strength.

As per Urban Dictionary, Megan Knees means when someone coordinates moving their knees, ass, and hips while moving up and down with the rhythm. The term originated from Megan Thee Stallion’s performances and her dances on stage. To be good at the Megan Knees Trend, one has to have immense strength in their legs.

The trend is one of the ways people have embraced their sexuality.

The Megan Knees Trend Challenge On TikTok

One of the first TikTokers to post the recent Megan Knees Trend was social media influencers @allisynmichelle, aka Allisyn, and @hoareboys, aka Shahnta Hoare. Shahnta did the trend holding her two kids.

TikToker @lovesoreal uploaded a video on the trend on September 11, and the video already has 6.5 million views and over 377.4k likes.

This isn’t the first challenge where people tried to see if they have the Megan Knees. Earlier this year, people made TikTok videos on the song Knees Like Megan, where they tried to dance like Megan Thee Stallion.

What Is The Megan Knees Trend Original Song?

The original song of The Megan Knees Trend is called Twinenem and was posted by artist Coi Leray on August 11, 2021, on TikTok. It has more than 10.7 million views and almost a million likes on TikTok. Many people found out about the song because of The Megan Knees challenge.

Coi Leray is an American rapper, singer, and songwriter known for her albums Now or Never, Everythingcoz, and EC2. She has more than 6.1 million followers and 102.3 million likes on TikTok. She is the daughter of the popular TV personality and rapper Benzino.
