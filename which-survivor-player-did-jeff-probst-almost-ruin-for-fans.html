Which Survivor player did Jeff Probst almost ruin for fans?

 Posted on July 25, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  103 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

After John Cochran played Survivor for the first time, he was a lovable underdog. Then, Cochran came back and won a season which was a moment of elation for many fans who thought he was great. However, things went awry after that when Jeff Probst began shoving Cochran down the fan’s throats. For example, Probst brought Cochran up during reunions for no reason. If that wasn’t enough, Probst also made Cochran a prize for a challenge. It was enough to make fans begin to resent Cochran. Thankfully, Probst stopped pushing Cochran down the fan’s throats which allowed them to like him again.
